Target Audience

The target audience is entrepreneurs, business owners, and professionals interested in growing their businesses and developing strong connections within Rosario. This site will also target those who seek to start their business and who seek to become a member of the chamber. Moreover, it targets those who want to know more about the city's history.

Personas

  • Persona 1: Liza is a 55-year-old businesswoman, close to retirement age, with strong ties in the community. She is married, has two children and three grandkids. She has a Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration and a Master of Science in Organizational Leadership. She has organizational leadership skills and finances since he has worked for 30 years with several important companies in the city. She has a computer to do simple tasks and a tablet that she takes everywhere to take her notes and use for work. She hopes to team up with other companies to expand her operation in the area.
  • Persona 2: Carlos is a single male entrepreneur in his early 25s, looking to connect with potential investors in his e-commerce business. He has two years of experience working in the specialized area of technology. He graduated from high school and has some college courses. He's highly proficient in computer skills because his laptop from him is his main work tool from him.

Scenarios

  1. How can I get in contact with potential investors for my growing business?
  2. What benefits does the local government has to offer to small and medium-sized enterprises?
  3. What paperwork is required to register a company in Rosario city?
  4. Where can I find business and commercial lawyers and accountants with great references?
  5. What are some local stories of successfully growing businesses?
