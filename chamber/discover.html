Industries

Port of Rosario

The Port of Rosario is a fluvial port with access from the Atlantic Ocean. It is one of the largest goods export centers in Argentina. It is located 550 km upriver from the Argentine sea, being the axis of the largest port area in the country known as Up-River.

National Capital of Football

In 2020, Rosario was declared the National Capital of Football. Two of the most important Argentine football clubs, Newell's and Rosario Central, are located in Rosario. The city became the third in the world (after Milan and Montevideo) to be home to two world championship teams when Independiente won the 1973 Intercontinental Cup.
